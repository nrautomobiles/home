The Shop

Family workshop.
Serious craft.

N R Automobiles has been running out of Mount Road, Chennai since 2020 — six years of stripped engines, rebuilt gearboxes, and Bullets that came in limping and left thumping properly again.

At the center of it is Latifuddin, who specializes almost entirely in Royal Enfield Bullets — the older carbureted classics as much as the newer ones. Not a general garage that "also does bikes." A guy who's spent years learning exactly how this one machine breaks, and exactly how to put it back together right.

Why people keep coming back 860+ ratings on Justdial, 4.9/5 average, and a Google listing sitting past 1000 five-star reviews. That's not luck — that's repeat riders sending their friends.
